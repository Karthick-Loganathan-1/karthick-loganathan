Research Projects

TiO₂ Thin-Film UV Protection

Post-deposition nano-imprinting to enhance UV-resistance on flexible polymer substrates. Presented at ICONN 2025 under Dr. P. Malar.

STT Simulation using Heusler Alloys

MTJ modeling using NEGF and tight-binding Hamiltonians derived from DFT data. Supervised by Dr. Banerjee at MAVENs Lab.

Quaternary Heusler Alloy Tuning (Ongoing)

DFT-based study of electronic structure and magnetic properties of substituted Heusler alloys using Quantum ESPRESSO and SPR-KKR.

ML Predictor for Heusler Alloy Bandgaps (Ongoing)

Developing a machine learning model to predict bandgaps based on compositional and structural features. Uses scikit-learn and DFT-derived data.

Skills

  • DFT Simulations: Quantum ESPRESSO, SPR-KKR
  • Quantum Transport: NEGF, Kwant
  • Programming: Python, Bash, Fortran
  • Machine Learning: scikit-learn, regression/classification models
  • Data Processing & Visualization: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Experimental: Thin-film deposition, XRD, AFM, UV-Vis, SEM
